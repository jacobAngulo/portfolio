Recent Work

RxID

RxID is an app that allows users to track, manage, identify, and log their experiences with their medications. The biggest feature of RxID was it's ability to identify a medication by image. I assisted with the implementation of Oauth in our app, built out and added functionality to the scheduling pill dosage pages, built the landing page and styled all my front end contributions to be pixel perfect.

Tech used: React, Redux, Node, Express, SASS
Deployed site: www.rxid.net
Repo: github.com/labs12-rxid
